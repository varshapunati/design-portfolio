VARSHA PUNATI

Dear Reader,

Welcome, my name is Varsha Punati. A quick introduction about me, I am and always have been a creative. My creative avenues stretch from graphic design to classical dance to photography. I have always felt a draw towards the arts. And with that draw to art came a desire to perfect my own work. Graphic design is ones of my few high risk, high reward hobbies, and with my time at CSU Monterey Bay, I have been lucky enough to expand that into a career. Through hours of experimental passion and through the kindness of my many professors I have had the ability to hone my skills across various design applications, including, but not limited to Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, as well as Figma. I have also worked with many website hosting platforms and have mastered web design. My full trajectory as a designer has been highlighted in my resume.


Apart from the creative lanes of graphic design, I am a passionate outdoorsman. I grew up fortunate enough to explore many of the world's beauties. In 2019 I summited Mt. Kilimanjaro and thus began my mountain climbing journey. I have since trekked Everest Base Camp, Mont Blanc, climbed El Capitan, Half Dome, and Mt. Dana, as well asMt. Whitney. I continue to search for peaks to climb and one day hope to scale the Seven Summits. Through my mountain climbing I have gained many lifeskills including leadership, cooperation, and perseverance, which help me on the slopes but also in my professional life. I share a more in depth analysis of my experiences in my TEDx Talk.
